Welcome to Fluffy Friends, a small group of dedicated dog lovers that provide daycare, grooming, and training services.From the heart of dublin we are here to provide the best care for your bestfriends!! Established over 5 years ago, Fluffy Friends is run by an expertly trained team, covering the whole of the North of Dublin. Our teams love and care for animals drives their passion to provide an excellent service that goes above and beyond.

Fluffy Friends gives each of our guests the most positive and unique experience no matter what service they are enjoying: Daycare, Boarding, Grooming or Training. We not only have the most unique facility in the county with over 25 exercise yards, but we simply do things differently. We have 3 certified vet assistants on staff and are the most vet recommended facility in the county.
